Aminal VRGDA Love Curve

Summary

The VRGDA (Variable Rate Gradual Dutch Auction) creates a smooth curve that incentivizes feeding hungry Aminals while discouraging overfeeding.

Energy Level Love Multiplier Zone Incentive
<0.005 ETH 10x Starving Maximum reward for rescuing neglected Aminals
0.005-0.1 ETH 9.5x-7.4x Hungry Strong incentive to feed low-energy Aminals
0.1-1 ETH 7.4x-5.5x Fed Good returns encourage regular interaction
1-10 ETH 5.5x-3.5x Well-Fed Natural equilibrium zone
10-100 ETH 3.5x-0.1x Overfed Diminishing returns discourage overfeeding
>100 ETH 0.1x Extreme Severe penalty prevents wasteful feeding
